MYVOTE

VOTER RESOURCE HUB FOR CIVIC ENGAGEMENT

OVERVIEW

MYVOTE IS A COMPREHENSIVE VOTER RESOURCE HUB DEVELOPED COLLABORATIVELY TO EMPOWER CITIZENS WITH ESSENTIAL VOTING INFORMATION. THE PLATFORM PROVIDES ACCESSIBLE RESOURCES FOR FINDING LOCAL CANDIDATES, UNDERSTANDING BALLOT MEASURES, AND SIMPLIFYING THE VOTER REGISTRATION PROCESS. WITH A FOCUS ON USABILITY AND VISUAL CLARITY, MYVOTE MAKES CIVIC PARTICIPATION MORE ACCESSIBLE TO ALL VOTERS.

KEY FEATURES

  • CANDIDATE FINDER — SEARCHABLE DATABASE OF LOCAL CANDIDATES WITH DETAILED PROFILES
  • VOTER REGISTRATION GUIDE — STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS FOR VOTER REGISTRATION BY STATE
  • BALLOT INFORMATION — COMPREHENSIVE EXPLANATIONS OF BALLOT MEASURES AND PROPOSITIONS
  • POLLING LOCATION FINDER — LOCATE NEARBY POLLING PLACES AND VOTING TIMES
  • RESPONSIVE DESIGN — OPTIMIZED FOR DESKTOP AND MOBILE DEVICES
  • ACCESSIBILITY FOCUS — DESIGNED WITH CLEAR INFORMATION HIERARCHY AND READABLE TYPOGRAPHY

DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

LED FRONT-END DEVELOPMENT AND UX DESIGN FOR THE MYVOTE PROJECT, CRAFTING INTUITIVE LAYOUTS THAT PRIORITIZE ACCESSIBILITY AND VISUAL CLARITY. USED FIGMA FOR WIREFRAMING AND PROTOTYPING TO ENSURE A USER-CENTERED DESIGN PROCESS. THE RESULTING WEBSITE PROVIDES A SMOOTH, USER-FRIENDLY EXPERIENCE THAT REMOVES BARRIERS TO VOTER ENGAGEMENT AND PROMOTES INFORMED CIVIC PARTICIPATION.
